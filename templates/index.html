How It Works

1. Invite the Bot

Add the translator bot to any public channel where you expect Scandinavian languages to be used.

2. Send a Message

A team member writes a message in Norwegian, Swedish, or Danish in the monitored channel.

3. Get the Translation

The bot instantly posts a clean, formatted translation in a separate channel for clarity.
